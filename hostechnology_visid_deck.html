Usage Guidelines

Clear Space: Maintain minimum clear space around logo equal to the height of one diamond.

Minimum Size: Never reproduce logo smaller than 120px width for digital, 1 inch for print.

Backgrounds: Primary logo works best on light backgrounds. Use sufficient contrast for readability.

Don'ts: Never rotate, skew, or distort the logo. Never change colors outside approved palette. Never add effects or shadows.

Tone of Voice

Knowledgeable but not arrogant. We share technical expertise in clear, accessible language. We meet clients where they are and build solutions around their existing workflows.

Optimistic but realistic. We believe technology can multiply human capacity without replacing humans. We acknowledge challenges while focusing on achievable solutions.

Professional but warm. We're serious about quality work, but friendly in how we communicate. We use "we" language to emphasize collaboration.
