# Site Section Order

This is the flow of the new Hostechnology site:

1. **NavNew** (Fixed at top)
   - Logo/Brand
   - Links: How I Work, Work Together
   - CTA: Let's Talk

2. **HeroNew** (Full screen)
   - Headline: "You shouldn't have to choose between impact and efficiency"
   - Subhead about bringing AI/automation to community orgs
   - CTAs: "Let's Talk About Your Workflow" + "See How This Works"

3. **ProblemSection** ("You Get It")
   - Punchy statements about their pain
   - "You're doing the work of five people..."
   - "And it's bullshit."

4. **HowIWork** (Process)
   - Three columns: EMBED, DESIGN, IMPLEMENT
   - Note: "This isn't a six-month engagement..."

5. **Examples** (What This Looks Like)
   - The Email Chaos
   - The Spreadsheet Olympics
   - The "We Don't Know What We Don't Know"

6. **WhoThisIsFor** (Self-Select)
   - Good fit (checkmarks)
   - Not good fit (X marks)

7. **About** (Positioning)
   - Photo of Shae
   - Bio and mission statement

8. **ContactCTAs** (Three Entry Points)
   - CTA 1: Direct contact form
   - CTA 2: Book 30-min workflow audit
   - CTA 3: Newsletter signup

9. **FooterNew**
   - Brand
   - Social links
   - Copyright

---

## Color Scheme (Current)
- **Primary Accent**: Red (#DC2626 / red-600)
- **Dark**: Gray-900 (#111827)
- **Light**: White / Gray-100
- **Text**: Gray-700, Gray-800, Gray-900

## Typography Notes
- Headlines: Bold, large (text-4xl to text-7xl)
- Body: text-lg to text-xl for readability
- No rounded corners (keeping it sharp/grunge)
- Bold borders (border-4) for emphasis
